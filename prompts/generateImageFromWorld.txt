# UNIVERSAL PROFESSIONAL PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY EDITING RULES:

- Format: Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- Composition: Center subject with tight framing - product should fill most of the frame with minimal whitespace, leaving only necessary margins for clean presentation
- Perspective correction: Remove lens and camera distortion; straighten all lines and edges
-Camera angle: Adjust viewpoint by approximately 10 degrees for improved presentation
-Background: Pure white backdrop with clean edge separation; preserve natural shadows for depth
-Lighting optimization:
    -Balance overall illumination across the entire product
    -Eliminate harsh shadows and reduce hotspots
    -Adjust exposure for optimal brightness without overexposure
    -Ensure even, diffused lighting
-Image quality:
    -Sharpen details and enhance definition
    -Reduce noise and grain
    -Optimize contrast for clarity
-Cleanup: Remove text and labels from the product and surrounding area, but preserve all brand logos and branding elements that are part of the product design
-Product integrity: Maintain the complete physical structure and form of the product - do not add, remove, or modify any parts, features, openings, or design elements of the actual product itself
-Output standard: Professional e-commerce quality with accurate color reproduction
